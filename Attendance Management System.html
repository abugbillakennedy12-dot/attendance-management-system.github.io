System Rules

  • Teacher Marks: Teacher markings are FINAL and cannot be changed by students
  • Student Marks: Students can only mark their own attendance
  • Time Window: Students can only mark attendance from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM
  • Daily Limit: Students can only mark attendance once per day
  • Priority: If a teacher marks attendance, it overrides any student self-mark
